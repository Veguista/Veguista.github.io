About me

I am Pablo, a Technical Designer from Spain with 6+ years of demonstrable experience developing games in Unreal Engine, Unity, and my own Engines. I have also worked as a Gameplay Programmer, UX Designer, and Tools Developer.

Parallel to Game Development, I work as a Non-Formal-Educator and a Mediator. That field has helped me develop my soft skills, improving my capacity for self-reflection, my adaptability, and my proactivity.

I am searching for Technical Design and Gameplay Programming roles. I can work remotely or relocate within Spain and the UK.

Zulo Interactive

Lady Umbrella is an action-adventure 3rd person shooter in which players fight using a multifuncional umbrella. Use it to shoot like a shotgun, hook enemies, and divert bullets.

I developed the games' cover System and in-engine Tools. I also took ownership of the project's level optimization strategy, the creation and integration of VFXs, and contributed to the programming of other Gameplay Elements.

Pandora's Madness

Team-13

Pandora's Madness is a first-person horror game. In it, a museum’s security guard is chased by statues when he looks at them. The guard will try to escape the museum by avoiding the statues, solving puzzles, and using strange artifacts.

I designed the enemies' AI behavior and the game's environmental puzzles. I also worked as the team's producer.
